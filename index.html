What We Do

A3 Chow is a full-service event staffing agency that represents a variety of talented event staff including but not limited to;


Brand Ambassadors, Country Club Events, Private Weddings, Hotels Events, Servers and Bartenders.



The management team at our office have combined expertise of over 30 years of experience.

  • Brand Ambassadors

    Brand Ambassadors are often conceived as “key salesmen”. They are employed by a company or organization to help raise brand awareness and increase sales. They complete a variety of tasks ranging from promoting products or services in-store to implementing marketing campaigns. They are also known as influencers or corporate ambassadors.

  • Country Club Events

    Event coordinators control an event from conception to clean up. They meet with clients to work out event details, plan with the client and their team, scout and book locations, food, entertainment, staff and cleanup. Event coordinators make sure the shrimp cocktail stays cold and the hired band stays hot.

  • Private Weddings

    Bring the vision of brides and grooms to life by planning, organizing and overseeing all aspects of the wedding ceremony, reception and, often, the honeymoon. They ensure that the preparation, as well as the execution, of the couple’s big day goes smoothly and that the newlyweds are satisfied with the results.

  • Hotels Events

    When the guests arrive, they will be pleased to find your polished appearance and dedication to exceptional service that delights in the details. You will feel a sense of accomplishment knowing that you’ve impressed each guest with personalized attention.

  • Servers

    Servers are the first line of visible staff that the guests encounter during the special event. The servers’ ability to make the occasion an enjoyable one for the guests is paramount for the location that hired them, since it’s our reputation on the line as a great facility for functions.

  • Bartenders

    Prepare alcohol or non-alcohol beverages for bar and restaurant patrons. Interact with customers, take orders and serve snacks and drinks. Assess customers' needs and preferences and make recommendations. Mix ingredients to prepare cocktails.
